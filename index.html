About Me

Hello! I'm Sylvan, and this is the place where I showcase my personal brand and journey...

I am a graduate student at the University of Southern California, pursuing a Master of Science in Applied Communication Analysis with a focus on data analysis, strategic communication, and business intelligence. With advanced technical skills in data processing, I am dedicated to understanding how technology and communication shape societal trends and influence business strategies.

As a researcher with a solid academic and industry background, I aspire to provide valuable insights into the evolving relationship between technology, culture, and society. Currently, I am independently developing a personal project that aligns with my passion for innovation and creativity.

In my professional career as a project manager and analyst, I have led cultural research projects, presented at industry conferences, and developed brand strategies by integrating psychological and sociological insights. My work has also involved exploring emerging technologies, such as AI, to design digital personas and innovative business methodologies.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I am also a creator. I enjoy engaging in literary writing, writing book and movie reviews, as well as exploring photography and painting as forms of self-expression.

Feel free to explore my work and get in touch with me if you want to collaborate or just chat!

TravelNest

With SoloVoyage, travelers can easily plan trips, track expenses, mark destinations, and capture moments through photo uploads and journaling. Whether solo or with loved ones, it offers a seamless blend of planning and preserving experiences.

As an independent creator, my goal is to merge functionality with an elegant design, making every journey uniquely yours.
